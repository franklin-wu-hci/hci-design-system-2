Footer

I've opted for simplicity, focusing on a clean look with my name and copyright year centrally placed. It's important to me that the design is not only visually minimalistic but also highly accessible. I will ensure that the text contrast is stark against the background for visibility and that the font size is generous enough for readability. There will be no need for navigation links in this specific footer, keeping it straightforward and free from clutter.
